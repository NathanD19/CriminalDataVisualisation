Background and Motivation

Public safety is a major concern for all citizen of Victoria and allowing the public to access this data allows for the government and Victoria police to be transparent with the people they are protecting.

Project Objectives

With this visualisation, I am trying to identify trends and create useful representations of data that can be used by Victorian Police, Government Analysts and everyday people. The benefits of this will allow these users to easily find the information they need from a quick to access, well organised website that holds all detailed and clear visualisations.

Users can then make quicker informed decisions from these visualisations rather than have too sift through the data in a spreadsheet. Creating an accurate visualisation will always provide a better picture to the user.

Data Source

Victorian Crime Data has been sourced from Crime Statistics Agency (CSA): https://www.crimestatistics.vic.gov.au/

CSA is responsible for processing, analysing and publishing Victorian crime statistics, independent of Victoria Police.

The data sourced is a master excel with multiple tables that hold criminal incident data.
This data includes rates of crime types and crime sub groups, incident location data, family violence and incident investigation status
